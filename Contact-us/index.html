Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you get started?

Typically we can onboard a new client within 5–7 business days after an initial strategy call. We'll assess your current setup and build a roadmap before anything goes live.

Do you work with businesses outside Nigeria?

Absolutely. While we're based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, we work with clients across Africa and internationally. All collaboration happens remotely with regular check-ins.

What industries do you specialise in?

We've worked across 12+ industries including tech, real estate, healthcare, e-commerce, professional services, and education. Our systems are adaptable to any growth-focused business.

What does the Growth Partner Program include?

It's a fully managed growth engagement — strategy, paid ads, content, automation, funnels, and analytics — all under one roof. Think of it as your outsourced marketing department.

How do I book a free strategy call?

Click the "Book a Growth Call" button at the top of any page, or visit calendly.com/main-oeaglobal. It's free and takes 30 minutes.
