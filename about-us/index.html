Our Mission

To democratise world-class marketing for growth-focused businesses. We build and operate the systems that help you compete, and win, regardless of your size.

Our Vision

Our Story

OEA Global was founded by Olufemi Emmanuel Akindele after years of watching great businesses struggle not because of their product, but because of chaotic, disconnected marketing that never built real momentum.

We started with a single question: What if a business had access to a fully integrated growth team powered by AI and strategy? That question became our obsession, and OEA Global was born.

Today, we partner with startups, SMEs, and fast-growing brands across Africa and beyond to build marketing systems that compound over time, attract the right customers, and scale predictably.
